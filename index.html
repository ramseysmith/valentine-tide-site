The Tide Comes in Black

Valentine Tide is surf for the after-hours — born where the last light drowns and the water turns to ink. We make apparel for the ones who paddle out under a dead moon, who find the calm somewhere past the break, in the cold and the quiet and the dark.

No sun-bleached clichés. No pastel postcards. Just heavy threads cut for restless souls — gothic lines, salt-worn black, and a red that runs like the tide. This is the first wave. Surf the shadows with us.
